What We Do

Cakebun's has revolutionized online cake delivery experience and we are working every minute to make it better. This is our story:

Cakebuns.com, India's favourite online cake maker was founded in year 2017 by Rajesh Bhat. Created to offer world class online cake delivery experience to customers with most comprehensive choices, the company began its network with 20 major cities and now serving across 408 cities in India. It aimed to offer best value for money, best quality in product and services with a dedicated customer support.

After consolidating it's position in the market as a brand recognized for its reliability and quality, Cakebun's has added 28 countries to its service list and further growing.

Today Cakebuns.com is much more than an online florist, it's a one-stop-shop for your gifts and greetings. It offers the broadest handpicked selection of flowers, cakes, chocolates, fresh fruits, dry fruits, sweets and designer gifts.

Remaining reliable, efficient and at forefront of technology, Cakebun's is committed to customer satisfaction. We have trust and responsibility placed by thousands of happy customers, we promise to deliver up to their expectations with our world class customer support and offices in 7 cities across India. Cakebuns is there for you , whenever and wherever.
